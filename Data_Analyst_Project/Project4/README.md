# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive multi-page **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, and product performance. The dashboard provides actionable business insights using **Power Query**, **DAX**, and interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset containing order, customer, product, payment, and coupon information.

The dashboard is divided into three business-focused pages:

- 📈 Executive Dashboard
- 👥 Customer Analytics
- 📦 Product Performance

It enables stakeholders to monitor revenue, customer trends, product performance, payment preferences, and marketing effectiveness.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track revenue and order trends.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate coupon effectiveness.
- Understand payment method preferences.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Compare revenue contribution across products.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of business performance.

### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Average Cart Size

### Visualizations
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Order Status Distribution
- Payment Method Distribution
- Interactive Filters

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Analytics

Focuses on customer acquisition and purchasing behavior.

### KPIs
- Total Customers
- Average Revenue per Customer
- Coupon Usage Percentage

### Visualizations
- Revenue by Referral Source
- Coupon Usage Distribution
- Top Customers by Revenue
- Revenue by Payment Method

---

## 3️⃣ Product Performance

Analyzes product-wise sales and revenue.

### KPIs
- Total Products Sold
- Average Unit Price
- Average Quantity per Order

### Visualizations
- Revenue by Product
- Quantity Sold by Product
- Revenue Share (Treemap)
- Monthly Product Revenue Trend
- Price vs Quantity Scatter Plot

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- Revenue can be monitored across different years using interactive filters.
- Referral sources contribute differently to overall sales performance.
- Coupon adoption rate provides insight into promotional campaign effectiveness.
- Product-wise revenue comparison helps identify top-performing products.
- Monthly revenue trends reveal seasonal sales patterns.
- Payment methods show varying customer preferences.
- Customer-level analysis highlights high-value customers.
